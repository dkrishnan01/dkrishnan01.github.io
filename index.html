About

My journey into the world of Design

  • 2016-2018

    M.S., Information Science at the University of Michigan

    A Master's degree in Information Science focusing on Human-Computer Interaction at the School of Information, University of Michigan (Ann Arbor) paved the way as I forayed into the world of Design. Inspired by large-scale leaps in technology such as IoT and Ubiquitous Technology, I wanted to do more than just code products others created. I wanted have a say in the way products are designed

  • 2013-2016

    Software Engineering Manager, Wipro Tecnologies

    At 25, I was the youngest manager in a team of over 100 people with 7 managers. Tasked with the day-to-day management of 3 teams with nearly 30 engineers, I learnt the ropes of leadership and injected fresh and youthful ideas into the organization. I pioneered innovation at every level in the engineering teams. As I searched for newer challenges, my desire for advanced education soon took over

  • 2010-2013

    Senior Software Engineer, Wipro Tecnologies

    An opportunity to join the tech world came in the form of a software engineering job right out of college at Wipro Technologies in India. I worked on developing card and mobile payment products using Java and Javascript. 3 years in, my hard work was recognized and I was given a huge promotion
